Website Evaluations

GB Electrical Services

This website connects you with a great company to fix your electrical problems.

  • Contrast:

    The contrast of this site is very good. Only four colors are used: white, blue, black, and yellow. It doesn't strain your eyes, allows you to easily read and follow along to find what your looking for.

  • Repetition:

    The repetition of this site is very good. The colors and sizings stay consistent through each page. The header nav bar is the same on each page allowing for easy navigation.

  • Alignment:

    The alignment of this site is very good. There is consistent spacing with images and text. Everything stays in line and is easy to read.

  • Proximity:

    The proximity of this site is very good. The text is close to the image that references the context. Each box is evenly spaced with one another allowing for easy readability.

Yale Art

This website provides information about the art program at Yale University.

  • Contrast:

    The contrast of this site is very poor. The colors clash with one another and make difficult to read. The background image repeats, causing you to have to much to focus on.

  • Repetition:

    The repetition of this site is very poor. The colors and sizings of text are all over the place. Each page changes the layout and makes it difficult to navigate.

  • Alignment:

    The alignment of this site is very poor. Everything is aligned to the left side of the page causing crowding.

  • Proximity:

    The proximity of this site is very poor. The text boxes are on top of one another. No consistent spacing between its elements.
