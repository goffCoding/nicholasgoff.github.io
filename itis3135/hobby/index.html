My Hobby

One of my main hobbies is gaming. I started playing video games with my older brother when I was younger. I remember playing games on our ps2 and wii, and then eventually games on the xbox 360. The games that stick out to me the most playing were halo 3, halo reach, wii sports, plants vs zombies, and skylanders. This was the start of my love for video games. My go to game currently when I have some free time is Apex Legends. I have played many games through the years that I have really enjoyed such as call of duty, battlefield, rocket league, rainbow six siege, and many more. A current favorite at the moment is Split Fiction. Gaming eventually led to me getting into computers and technology.

How I Game

I usually play competitive modes in video games because they offer a greater challenge and a stronger sense of accomplishment. The ranking system adds extra motivation, pushing me to improve and strive for higher ranks rather than just playing to win. However, I also enjoy casual modes when I don't have the time to commit to a full competitive match or when I just want a more relaxed gaming experience.
